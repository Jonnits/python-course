# Task 2.5 - Frontend Inspirations

## Chosen Applications and Perceived Strengths

### 1. [Umami](https://www.umami.recipes/)

- Clean, tasteful color palette (no pun intended).
- Simple UI showcases recipes efficiently, without overpiling information.
- Clean, easy-to-navigate folder structure.

### 2. [Paprika Recipe Manager](https://www.paprikaapp.com/)

- Different but consistent displays for different devices.
- Noticeable, bold header color aids good brand recognition.
- Simple use of images and blurring keeps appearance clean.

### 3. [ReciMe](https://www.recime.app/)

- Great use of colors and fonts make it feel familiar but professional.
- Strong interconnectivity with other popular apps.
- Simple, clever logo and transitions add to sleek impression.
